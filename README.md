# FinalFlagger

Decision safety platform to prevent risky actions using deterministic rule-based validation.

**Website: https://finalflagger.com**

---

## What is FinalFlagger?

FinalFlagger is a deterministic decision-safety platform. It sits between a person and any irreversible, life-altering decision they are about to make — and tells them, clearly, whether a known dangerous pattern exists in what they are about to sign.

People routinely make decisions that permanently damage their careers, finances, and legal standing. These failures are not random. They repeat the same patterns: unfair employment bonds, predatory loan clauses, one-sided cancellation penalties, unlimited liability traps, vague placement-linked agreements. FinalFlagger detects those patterns before you cross the point of no return.

The platform uses generative AI to read and understand documents, extract explicit facts, and pass those facts to a rule engine of 200+ deterministic safety rules. Every flag is traceable to the exact clause and rule that triggered it. No opinions. No AI guesses. Just a clear, auditable verdict.

---

## The Verdict System

Every analysis returns exactly one of three outputs:

**✅ SAFE** — No known dangerous pattern was detected. Does not mean the document is perfect. It means no hard rule was violated.

**⚠️ RISKY** — One or more concerning patterns were found. The document is not blocked, but specific clauses need attention before signing.

**❌ BLOCKED** — A known hard-rule violation exists. Signing as-is carries serious, documented risk. Does not mean reject — it means negotiate first.

---

## How it Works

1. **Upload your document** — PDF, Word, or plain text. Any binding contract or agreement.
2. **AI extracts the facts** — Generative AI reads the document and extracts explicit facts. No interpretation. No advice.
3. **Rules evaluate the facts** — 200+ deterministic safety rules check the extracted facts against known dangerous patterns.
4. **You get a verdict** — SAFE, RISKY, or BLOCKED. Every flag is linked to the exact clause and rule that caused it.
5. **Document is deleted** — Your document is removed after analysis. The verdict is yours to keep.

## Real Scenarios

### Employment — Job Offer with a Service Bond
A software engineer in Pune receives an ₹8 LPA offer. The offer letter contains a 2-year service bond with a ₹2.5 lakh penalty for early exit. There is no definition of what training was provided. No exit clause. FinalFlagger flags it **BLOCKED** — financial commitment without exit clause is a hard rule violation. The engineer negotiates the bond down to 6 months before joining.

### Education — Ed-Tech Placement-Linked Bond
A student enrolls in a ₹1.2 lakh full-stack bootcamp in Bengaluru. The agreement says she must pay 15% of her first-year CTC if she gets placed "through the program" — without defining what that means. FinalFlagger flags it **BLOCKED** — undefined trigger in a financial obligation is a known predatory pattern.

### Rental — Bengaluru Leave & Licence Agreement
A tenant signs an 11-month agreement in HSR Layout for ₹28,000/month. Clause 9 allows the landlord to raise rent by "up to 20% at their discretion" after 6 months. Clause 12 forces arbitration at a location chosen by the landlord. FinalFlagger flags it **RISKY** — uncapped discretionary rent hike and one-sided arbitration clause.

### Finance — NBFC Personal Loan with Prepayment Penalty
A borrower takes a ₹5 lakh personal loan at 14% from a fintech NBFC. Clause 18 adds a 4% prepayment penalty. Clause 19 allows the lender to change the interest rate with 30 days' notice, with no cap on the change. FinalFlagger flags it **BLOCKED** — uncapped variable rate without exit clause violates the deterministic safety rule for financial commitments.

### Business — Freelance Contract with Unlimited Liability
A freelance designer in Delhi signs a ₹90,000 branding contract. The agreement makes the freelancer "liable for all losses including indirect and consequential losses, without limitation." No liability cap. No force majeure clause. FinalFlagger flags it **BLOCKED** — unlimited liability is a non-negotiable hard rule violation.

### Real Estate — Under-Construction Property in Noida
A buyer books a ₹68 lakh 3BHK. The builder agreement ties possession to "36 months from approval of building plan" — not from the booking date. The buyer faces a 20% cancellation penalty, but the builder has no corresponding penalty for delays. FinalFlagger flags it **RISKY** — unanchored possession date and one-sided cancellation clause.

## What FinalFlagger is NOT

- **Not a lawyer.** FinalFlagger never tells you what to do.
- **Not an AI opinion machine.** No "this might be risky" guesses.
- **Not a contract summariser.** It does not summarise — it flags.
- **SAFE does not mean the document is good.** It means no hard rule was violated.
- **BLOCKED does not mean reject.** It means a known dangerous pattern exists — negotiate.
